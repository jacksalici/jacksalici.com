“Innovation for Legacy” is the title of the new strategic plan of the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, written by the Rector of the University together with governance bodies and designed by me with Affinity by Canva. The plan is a comprehensive document that outlines the university's vision, mission, and strategic objectives for the next six years.
